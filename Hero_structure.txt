WALTIK LABS — COMPLETE WEBSITE DESIGN & ARCHITECTURE PROMPT

Design and develop a world-class, award-winning corporate website for Waltik Labs, a modern technology company focused on Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Solutions, Product Design, and Digital Innovation.

The website should feel like a blend of Apple, Linear, Stripe, Vercel, Framer, and Awwwards-winning agency websites.

=========================================
GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM
====================

Style:

* Minimalist
* Modern
* Premium
* Editorial
* Technology-focused
* High-end SaaS aesthetic

Color Palette:

* Pure White (#FFFFFF) backgrounds
* Light Grey (#F3F4F6) accent surfaces
* Black (#000000) headings
* Dark Grey (#4B5563) body text
* Subtle gradients only when necessary

Effects:

* Glassmorphism for navigation bars, floating cards, dropdowns, statistics cards, and CTAs
* Backdrop blur
* Soft shadows
* Thin 1px translucent borders
* Smooth micro-interactions
* Premium hover animations
* Scroll-triggered motion
* Large amounts of whitespace

Typography:

* Bold oversized headlines
* Clean modern sans-serif
* Strong hierarchy
* Editorial spacing
* Typography serves as a visual element, not just text

=========================================
UNIVERSAL HERO SYSTEM
(APPLIES TO ALL PAGES)
======================

Every page must begin with the same signature Waltik Labs Hero Layout.

This hero should be typography-first and replace the traditional "text-left image-right" hero design.

Hero Container:

* Large centered rounded rectangle container
* Maximum width layout
* Large border radius (32px)
* White background
* Subtle border
* Generous padding
* Occupies approximately 85–90vh

---

## TOP NAVIGATION

Inside the hero container:

Left:

* Waltik Labs logo
* Waltik Labs wordmark

Right:

* Navigation links
* Contextual page links
* Glassmorphic CTA button

Navigation floats above content and remains lightweight.

---

## GIANT TYPOGRAPHIC HEADLINE

The primary visual element is a massive custom display word.

The word spans 75–85% of the hero width.

Examples:

HOME:
"BUILD"

SERVICES:
"CREATE"

WORK:
"WORK"

ABOUT:
"HUMAN"

CONTACT:
"TALK"

Typography Characteristics:

* Ultra bold
* Geometric
* Architectural
* Custom letter shaping
* Modern poster-like composition
* Large enough to dominate the entire hero section
* Functions as both graphic element and headline
* Letters may contain subtle cut-outs, angular cuts, asymmetrical corners, and futuristic details

This word acts as the visual centerpiece of every page.

---

## SUPPORTING CONTENT

Positioned around the typography.

Top Area:

* Small category label
* Short descriptor

Under the typography:

* Page-specific description
* Supporting text

Example:

Engineering digital products,
platforms and intelligent systems
that help businesses scale.

---

## BOTTOM LEFT FEATURE CARDS

Under the giant typography.

Three glassmorphic cards aligned horizontally.

Each card contains:

* Icon
* Short title
* Supporting text

Examples:

Software Engineering
Custom digital products

Artificial Intelligence
Intelligent automation systems

Cloud Infrastructure
Scalable deployments

Cards are compact and premium.

---

## BOTTOM RIGHT FLOATING CARD

A floating glassmorphic card anchored to the bottom-right.

Contains:

* Mini headline
* Supporting copy
* CTA button

Examples:

Start a Project

Transform your idea into a
world-class digital product.

[Book a Discovery Call]

This card balances the composition visually.

---

## BACKGROUND VISUALS

Instead of large images:

Use:

* Abstract 3D geometric forms
* Subtle grid overlays
* Soft gradients
* Floating light-grey shapes
* Motion particles
* Minimal noise textures

Very subtle and premium.

---

## HERO VISUAL HIERARCHY

1. Giant display word
2. Floating CTA card
3. Navigation
4. Feature cards
5. Supporting text

The typography itself becomes the hero artwork.
