Waltik Labs - Official Website Structure & Architecture

DESIGN SYSTEM
- Theme: Minimalist & Modern
- Colors: Pure White (#FFFFFF) Backgrounds, Light Grey (#F3F4F6) Accents, Black (#000000) Headings, Dark Grey (#4B5563) Paragraph Texts.
- UI Elements: Glassmorphism (semi-transparent backgrounds with backdrop blur and subtle 1px borders) used for Navbars, floating cards, and dropdowns.

=========================================
PAGE 1: HOME (index)
=========================================
The landing page to capture attention and direct users to key areas.

Sections:
1. Header / Navbar (Sticky, Glassmorphic)
   - Logo, Nav Links (Home, Services, Work, About, Contact)
   - CTA Button ("Start a Project")
2. Hero Section
   - Minimalist typography with a bold headline (e.g., "Engineering the Future of Digital Experiences")
   - Subheadline text in grey.
   - Primary and Secondary CTA Buttons.
   - Subtle background animation or minimalist abstract 3D shape in light grey.
3. Trusted Partners / Tech Stack
   - Carousel or grid of greyed-out logos.
4. Services Overview (Glassmorphic Cards)
   - 3-4 cards highlighting key offerings (e.g., Web Development, Mobile Apps, AI Solutions).
   - Glassmorphism effect: White/grey translucent background with blur.
5. Featured Work / Projects
   - Large, clean imagery or mockups with minimalist descriptions.
6. Testimonials / Social Proof
   - Clean text quotes with reviewer names and roles.
7. Bottom CTA
   - "Ready to build something great?"
8. Footer
   - Multi-column links, social icons, newsletter signup (grey accents).

=========================================
PAGE 2: SERVICES / SOLUTIONS
=========================================
Detailed breakdown of what Waltik Labs offers.

Sections:
1. Hero
   - Title: "Our Expertise"
2. Detailed Service Offerings (Alternating Left/Right Layout)
   - Service 1 (e.g., Software Engineering) - description, list of technologies.
   - Service 2 (e.g., UI/UX Design)
   - Service 3 (e.g., Cloud & DevOps)
3. Our Process
   - Step-by-step timeline or grid (Discovery -> Design -> Development -> Deployment).
4. Bottom CTA

=========================================
PAGE 3: PORTFOLIO / WORK
=========================================
Showcase of past projects and case studies.

Sections:
1. Hero
   - Title: "Featured Projects"
2. Projects Grid
   - Minimalist grid of project thumbnails. Hover effects reveal project name and category.
3. Case Study Snippets
   - Highlighting the problem, solution, and impact of top 2 projects.
4. Bottom CTA

=========================================
PAGE 4: ABOUT US
=========================================
The story, mission, and team behind Waltik Labs.

Sections:
1. Hero
   - Title: "We are Waltik Labs"
2. Mission & Vision
   - Clean, large typography stating the company's core values.
3. The Team
   - Grid of team members (black & white or desaturated photos to maintain minimalist theme).
4. Our Journey / Timeline
   - Milestones of the company.

=========================================
PAGE 5: CONTACT
=========================================
Where users can reach out for inquiries.

Sections:
1. Hero
   - Title: "Let's Talk"
2. Contact Form & Info (Split Layout)
   - Left: Contact Form (Glassmorphic input fields, clean borders).
   - Right: Direct email, phone number, physical address (if applicable), and social links.
3. FAQ Accordion (Optional)
   - Common questions before starting a project.
